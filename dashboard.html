Understanding PCOS/PCOD

What is PCOS?

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome is a hormonal disorder affecting reproductive-aged women, characterized by irregular periods, excess male hormones, and polycystic ovaries.

Management Tips
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Exercise regularly (30 mins daily)
  • Follow a balanced, low-glycemic diet
  • Manage stress through meditation
  • Get adequate sleep (7-8 hours)
When to See a Doctor

Consult a healthcare provider if you experience irregular periods for more than 3 months, excessive hair growth, severe acne, or difficulty conceiving.
